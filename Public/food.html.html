Best Foods in Bhutan

Top 3 you must try Bhutan food recommended by Karma Dorji

Chicken Curry

The dish comes with a chicken cooked with pandan leaves and ginger and spicy chilli,and ginger paste dipping sauces.Add some capsicum and green veggies as well to compelet your meal.

Ema Dastse

Choice of chilli and cheese(datse).The cheese is more of a sauce/gravy rather than of a souffle consistency and the chillies are not a seasoning but the main vegetable itself.

Kawa Datse

It is made with potato and cheese(Datse),cut into cubes,and put some chilli.Slowly stir until cheese melts and spread evenly and garnish dish wit red pepper flakes.
